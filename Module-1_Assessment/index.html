About Susan

Susan Ashmore completed her bachelor’s in mathematics and music performance with minors in physics, history, and social sciences at Brevard College in 2018. She will have her master’s in music in May of 2020. Though it appears Susan does nothing but study and play the oboe, she finds time to partake in other hobbies.

When the inspiration strikes, she is crafting a digital masterpiece in photoshop, or working on that novel she hopes to someday publish. Along with fine arts, creating curiosities in the kitchen, and playing with her hedgehog, Susan’s interests drift into space, literally! She spent the summer of 2019 working at the Pisgah Astronomical Research Institute teaching high school students about the joys of astrophysics and astrobiology. The coolest part of the job by far was operating the big radio telescopes. Who doesn’t want to point one of those things at Mars while it’s in retrograde?
